WavyTown Studios, LLC is a media production house that pushes for inclusive art communities through creative placemaking and media production.

Our programming puts culture at the forefront, uncovering voices from all levels of creators.
We’re currently developing Over Yondr, a docu-web series that reveals the intimate moments in an artist’s life while preparing for a creative production. Releasing March xx, Over Yondr will be hosted on the WavyTown Youtube page and will follow SoundCloud superstars bbno$, BelisGo, and others. If you’re in tune with underground hip hop, then you won’t want to miss this series.
