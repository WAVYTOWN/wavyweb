Australian professional haircare producer, launched a 100% Australian keratin based hair straightening range of products with the help of Dinergy. We started with a competitive analysis and a name generation for the products. From there, branding and packaging design was applied to eight core shampoos and conditioners. Website and business to business communication material was created to push the product through to salons countrywide. In-store point of sale and collateral was developed to support sales agents with seasonal promotional campaigns. Dinergy also created an expo stand to launch the range to the trade. Bhave now comprises over 100 shampoos, conditioners, styling products, treatments, colours + tints and salon accessories. With Dinergy at it's side, bhave is now an international brand with sales in the US and Europe and India. We have loved the process and are proud to have been associated with this great project.
