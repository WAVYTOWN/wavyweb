STAR+ROSE are leaders in bath, spa and body pampering in Australia. Dinergy has worked with them for eleven years to unify and promote their formidable range of locally and imported products into a strong, single-minded brand. Dinergy has product developed/sourced, designed and marketed over 500 individual products ranges from concept to execution. The services supplied are wide ranging:
1. corporate branding
2. seasonal product concept development
3. design
4. product sourcing
5. production + supplier management
6. presentations to major retailers
7. cost estimating
8. marketing + advertising
9. all web development + product specific micro-sites
10. trade show/EXPO design + production
